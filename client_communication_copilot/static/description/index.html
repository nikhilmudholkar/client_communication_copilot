Align all stakeholders and update your systems with the latest status of your customer orders

  • Manually updating B2B orders with customer requests from emails is a daunting task.

  • Sales managers have to retrieve the relevant quotations and sale orders, check what changed, and then update Odoo. Doing this correctly for every customer communication can be a time-consuming and error-prone process.

  • Our ChatGPT-powered Odoo app automates this entire process, saving sales managers hours of time and effort. It reads and understands customer emails, extracts the relevant information, and then assists users on how to update Odoo (quantities, dates, specifications, prices). It also notifies the relevant stakeholders.

  • The app is embedded in the Odoo chat box so sales managers can continue using the interface they are used to.
